Communicate with ease

Remote SMS Operations support.

Most of the functions of Irotect can be triggered by SMS commands from Trusted Contacts configured in the app. The app instantly reacts to the commands which helps in quick and accurate execution.

Just send SMS to the mobile with the codes referring to specific function and let the app do the work.

Scheduling when you want

Custom scheduling of tasks for the ease of user.

The app comes with ability to periodicly notify the trusted contacts about phone conditions and location.

You can schedule the app to send periodic sms about phone Location, Battery etc. By doing this you can keep an protective eye on your loved ones.

Emergency Call

Emergency Call for quick SOS.

The app comes with a Emergency Call feature which can be triggered by calling to "252525". This is the only feature of the app which is not triggered by sms.

This Emergency Call quickly sends sms to your trusted contact asking them to call you as soon as possible and within next 5 min they will recieve another message containing your Location, battery status with Other device info. It also changes the phone's state to stolen, takes periodic selfies and emails them to your security email periodicly.

New Sim Notifier

Notifies about sim card change or new sim card inserted.

This feature is added to the app as security reasons so that you can access your device even if somebody has tempted with your sim card.

Whenever the sim card in the device running irotect is re-inserted or the sim state changes the app noticies the first trusted contact about the change through sms reporting about the sim state change and new sim number the device is running right now.
