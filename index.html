Communicate with ease

Remote SMS Operations support.

Most of the functions of Irotect can be triggered by SMS codes from Trusted Contacts configured in the phone.

Just send SMS to the mobile with the codes referring to specific function and let the app do the work.

Scheduling when you want

Custom scheduling of tasks for the ease of user.

The app comes with ability to periodicly notify the trusted contacts about phone conditions and location.

You can schedule the app to send periodic sms about phone Location, Battery etc. By doing this you can keep an protective eye on your loved ones.

Panic Button

Panic Button for emergency situations.

The app comes with a panic button which can be accessed from your favourite launcher or quick launch button.

The panic button quickly sends sms to yout trusted contact containing your Location, battery status with custom message. It also changes the phone's state to stolen, takes periodic selfies and emails them to your security email.

You just have to send sms to your phone from one of the trusted contacts you specified earlier with sms body containing specific function codes, which are then processed by the app and the result is sent back to the trusted contact as sms.
